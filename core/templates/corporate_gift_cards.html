Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Can I use this to send Gift Cards to my friends and family?

A1. No, Corporate Gifting is exclusively for businesses to gift in bulk. To send Gift Cards to your loved ones, visit the E-Gift Card section.

Q2. Do you have minimum quantity conditions?

A2. We do expect a bulk order of a minimum of 50. For orders below this, please use our mobile app.

Q3. Can I get custom gifting options as per my requirement?

A3. Yes. There are special occasions for which we make custom gifting offerings to suit your requirement. Please let us know what you have in mind, and we can begin the conversation with you.

Q4. How will my employees receive the card?

A4. Sent instantly to their official mail or phone. For physical gift cards, delivery timelines may vary.
